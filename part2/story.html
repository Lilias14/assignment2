The Choice

Once upon a time, there was a young boy named Jonathan who loved to explore the world beyond his village. One day, while wandering, he found himself at a crossroads. Two paths stretched out before him: one wide, smooth, and inviting; the other narrow, rugged, and mysterious.

At the crossroads, a wise old man sat with a peculiar smile. "To choose a path," the man said, "you must first choose a hand. In my right hand, I hold a clear, glowing stone that promises safety and ease. In my left hand, I hold a dark, shimmering stone that leads to challenge and discovery."

Jonathan faced a decision. Which path would he take?
